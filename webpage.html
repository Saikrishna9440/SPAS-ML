About SPAS

Revolutionizing Student Performance Analysis

The Student Performance Analysis System (SPAS) is a web-based application that leverages machine learning to analyze and predict student performance. Developed as part of a Bachelor of Technology project in Computer Science & Engineering with specialization in Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning, SPAS aims to bridge the gap between educators and students by providing actionable insights.

Traditional student management systems often restrict access to top management, leaving lecturers without the tools they need to effectively monitor and guide their students. SPAS changes this by providing lecturers with predictive capabilities to identify students who may need additional support.

The system uses classification techniques from data mining to analyze previous semester grades and mid-term examinations, generating predictions that help educators intervene early and students understand their areas for improvement.

Key Features

Performance Prediction

Our machine learning model predicts future student performance based on historical data, helping educators identify at-risk students early.

Personalized Insights

Students receive customized reports highlighting their strengths and weaknesses in specific subjects and topics.

Parent Notifications

Weekly performance reports are automatically generated and shared with parents to keep them informed about their child's progress.

Interactive Dashboards

Visual analytics dashboards provide clear insights into class performance trends and individual student progress.

Study Recommendations

The system suggests specific study materials and approaches based on each student's performance patterns.

Report Generation

Automatically generate detailed PDF reports for students, parents, and administrators with just one click.
